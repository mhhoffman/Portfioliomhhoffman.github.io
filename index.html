About Me

I am a mixed Japanese-American born and raised in San Diego, California. My passions are deeply rooted in the arts, education, and entertainment, with my academic focus being on theatre and history. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Theatre at UC San Diego, I maintain a cumulative GPA of 3.94, earning Provost Honors. As a theatre student, I understand the importance of a strong work ethic. I’ve had the opportunity to develop my skills in acting, costume design, playwriting, stage management, set design, as well as being a crew member. When I’m not performing, I enjoy attending live music shows and spending time with my loved ones.

Having studied acting on both the east and west coast, I've developed a variety of techniques over the years. I began my intensive education through the La Jolla Playhouse's 'Young Performers Conservatory' learning Chekhov. I later attended Rutgers University in New Jersey, at which I studied Meisner. Finally, I attended UC San Diego for my bachelor's degree in Theatre, at which I studied Stanislavski.
